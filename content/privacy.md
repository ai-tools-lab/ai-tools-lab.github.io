This site and its third-party partners may use cookies and similar tracking technologies for:

- **Analytics cookies** — to understand site usage and improve content
- **Advertising cookies** — to serve relevant ads via Google AdSense or similar services

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies.
